Kids Learning Center

New Approach to Kids Education

At Assumption Kids Day Care and Nursery School, we believe that every child is a unique individual, full of potential and curiosity. Our dedicated team of caring educators provides a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where your child can grow, explore, and discover the joy of learning. With a perfect balance of fun, creativity, and early childhood education, we are here to lay the foundation for lifelong success. Join us as we inspire little minds, build confidence, and create unforgettable memories—one smile at a time.

Play Ground

"Our vibrant playground is designed for fun and exploration, where children can develop their physical skills, engage in active play, and create lasting friendships in a safe and secure environment."

Music and Dance

"Music and dance are an integral part of our curriculum, helping children express their creativity, develop coordination, and build confidence, all while having fun and enjoying the rhythm of life."

Arts and Crafts

"Through arts and crafts, our little ones explore their imagination, fine-tune their motor skills, and create beautiful works of art, fostering both creativity and problem-solving abilities."

Safe Transportation

"We prioritize the safety and well-being of our children with reliable and secure transportation, ensuring that every journey to and from school is smooth, safe, and stress-free for both kids and parents."

Healthy food

"Our school provides nutritious, balanced meals to support the growth and well-being of each child, teaching the importance of healthy eating habits that will last a lifetime."

Educational Tour

"Our educational tours expose children to real-world experiences, broadening their horizons and providing hands-on learning in a fun and interactive way, fostering curiosity and a love for discovery."
